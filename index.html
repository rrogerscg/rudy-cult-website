The doctrine

A cult following for engineers who ship, refactor, and revere Rudy.

The Cult of Rudy Aceves is a harmless, highly opinionated gathering of software developers and engineers. We speak in tickets, think in edge cases, and believe every incident review is better with Rudy in the room.

Membership requires no robes, no chanting, and no production deploys on Friday. Bring clean diffs, decent variable names, and a willingness to treat Rudy's judgment as a surprisingly reliable design pattern.

Sacred tenets

The engineering values of the Rudy faithful

01

Read the Logs

Assume the stack trace has something to teach you. Rudy would check the evidence before naming the bug.

02

Respect the Review

Comments should sharpen the code, not the ego. The Rudy way is direct, useful, and mergeable.

03

Ship With Care

Move with urgency, keep the blast radius small, and leave the system easier to understand.
